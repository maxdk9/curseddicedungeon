Description 

Survive the card dungeon — or die trying. 35 hero classes, 110 monsters and 12 brutal bosses. No ads, ever
Cursed Dice Dungeon is a premium roguelike deckbuilder that combines dice-based combat, card battler tactics, and deep RPG strategy in a procedurally generated fantasy dungeon. Every run is different. Every decision matters.

In this singleplayer strategy game, you explore procedurally generated dungeon paths, flip cards to reveal enemies and treasures, and rely on the roll of your dice to survive brutal battles. Each run is different thanks to procedural generation, creating endless replay value for players who enjoy deep tactical gameplay.

DICE, CARDS, AND TACTICS

At the core of the game is a unique system combining dice mechanics with card-based combat. Battles unfold in turn-based combat, where you roll dice to determine the strength of your attacks and use strategic choices to manage risks.

Higher rolls deliver stronger attacks, but luck alone won’t save you. To survive the dungeon, you must carefully plan your actions, manage your resources, and anticipate the monsters’ counterattacks.

This mix of dice strategy, card combat, and turn-based tactics creates a system that is simple to learn yet full of depth. Each move matters, and every round becomes a puzzle of risk versus reward.

ROGUELIKE ADVENTURE WITH MEANINGFUL CHOICES

The dungeon is never the same twice. Each run presents new routes, enemies, and rewards. Choose which enemies to face, which treasures to claim, and which paths to follow through the dungeon.

Every decision shapes your strategy. Recruit companions, collect powerful artifacts, and combine abilities to create devastating synergies.

Because choices matter, every run tells a different story. Success depends on adapting to what the dungeon throws at you.

35+ UNIQUE HERO CLASSES

Discover more than 35 playable classes, each offering a completely different playstyle.

Some heroes focus on powerful attacks and aggressive tactics, while others rely on clever resource management, defensive strategies, or magical abilities.

Experiment with different builds, discover powerful combinations, and develop new strategies as you master the dungeon.

DEEP STRATEGY, SIMPLE RULES

Cursed Dice Dungeon combines elements from several beloved genres:

• Roguelike adventure with procedural generation
• Card game mechanics inspired by classic solitaire-style gameplay
• Dice-based combat that adds tension and unpredictability
• Turn-based strategy where every move counts

This blend of roguelike deckbuilder mechanics, card battler gameplay, and dice strategy creates a unique experience with high replay value.

A COLORFUL FANTASY DUNGEON

Explore a vibrant 2D fantasy world filled with monsters, demons, and magical powers. Face dangerous enemies, discover mysterious artifacts, and fight your way deeper into the cursed dungeon.

The game’s colorful style and accessible mechanics make it easy to jump in, while the challenging gameplay ensures that mastering the dungeon remains difficult and rewarding.
